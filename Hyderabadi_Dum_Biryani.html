Hyderabadi Biryani

About

Hyderabadi Biryani, also known as Hyderabadi-Dum-Biryani is a style of Biryani from Hyderabad, India. Made with Basmati rice and Chicken or Goat meat. It originated within the kitchens of the Nizams of Hyderabad. It is a combination of the Hyderabadi and Mughlai cuisines. Hyderabadi-Dum-Biryani is an important dish of the South Indian Cuisine.

It has a lot more aroma & flavors when compared to any other chicken biryani. This dum biryani tastes better than the restaurant style chicken biryani and is easy to make at home.

How to Make Hyderabadi Biryani

The biryani uses the dum pukt method of cooking and thus gets its name as Hyderabadi-Dum-Biryani.

Dum pukt just means that it is slow cooked. The biryani is cooked over low heat in a sealed pot.

The steam inside the pot helps the meat and the rice cook together.

Types of Hyderabadi Biryani

  • Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani
  • Hyderabadi Mutton Biryani
